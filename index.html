About Zealth

Zealth is an alternative rock band known for their powerful sound and electrifying performances. Formed in 2020, the band brings a mix of raw energy and heartfelt lyrics to their music, drawing inspiration from modern rock, indie, and classic genres. With a growing fan base, Zealth is ready to take the world by storm with their upcoming album release.
